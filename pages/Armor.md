## Armor
The Armor category adds various kinds of armor sets. Every armor set has to be crafted in an [Armor Forge](https://github.com/mrCookieSlime/Slimefun4/wiki/Armor-Forge)

* [Chain Armor](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Chain-Armor)
* [Damascus Steel Armor](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Damascus-Steel-Armor)
* [Gold Armor](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Gold-Armor)
* [Reinforced Armor](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Reinforced-Armor)
* [Gilded Iron Armor](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Gilded-Iron-Armor)
* [Hazmat Suit](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Hazmat-Suit)
* [Cactus Armor](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Cactus-Armor)
