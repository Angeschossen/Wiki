## Use

The Electric Gold Pan will turn any Gravel put into it into Sifted Ore. It has the same function as a [Gold Pan](https://github.com/TheBusyBiscuit/Slimefun4/wiki/Gold-Pan).
***
## Energy Usage

| Tier | Speed | Energy Usage | Buffer |
